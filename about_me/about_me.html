ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Derek Nakagawa, a current 3rd year student at UCLA studying Data Theory. Over the past few years, I have been honing my data science skills through some clubs at UCLA (Bruin Sports Analytics & DataRes) and individual projects. For both of these clubs, I hold leadership positions so I can not only work on my technical skills in the feild, but also develop some leadership skills as well. During my time at UCLA, I have worked on projects that required visualizations, supervised machine learning (linear & non-linear regression), unsupervised machine learning (k-means clustering), natural language processing, image processing (OCR Model), and many more data visualization techniques. I will continue to learn more and more data science techniques and share my skills through this website.
